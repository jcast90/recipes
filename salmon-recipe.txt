Honey Glazed Salmon

serving size: 4

4 6oz filet salmon
4tbls of honey
TT salt andd pepper
4 oranges

Pre-heat oven: 425F

Squeeze the juice out of 3 oranges. Use the 4th orange to cut up for garnish. Slice the orange (1/2in thick slices)

Coat the salmon in honey then salt and pepper the filet.

Sear in a sautee pan and bake in oven for 5-10min. Once it is fully cooked, garnish with orang slices on top.
